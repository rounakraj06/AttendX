About The Project

AttendX is a modern AI-based Student Attendance & Department Management System created as a Semester VI Major Project.

Project Overview

AttendX has been developed to simplify attendance, department notices, study materials and student management into one platform. The system is designed with a modern interface, responsive layout and easy navigation to provide a better experience for students as well as faculty members. This project is developed by the students of St. Columba's College under Vinoba Bhave University as our Final Year Major Project.
