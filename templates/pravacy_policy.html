Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Welcome to AttendX. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how AttendX collects, uses, stores and protects your information while using our website and attendance management system. By accessing or using AttendX, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

AttendX may collect information necessary to provide educational and attendance management services. The information may include:

  • Student Name
  • Roll Number
  • Department and Semester
  • Email Address (if available)
  • Attendance Records
  • Faculty Information
  • Basic device and browser information for security purposes

2. How We Use Your Information

The collected information is used only for academic and administrative purposes. This includes maintaining attendance records, generating reports, improving system performance, providing support, and ensuring secure access to the platform.

3. Face Attendance Data

AttendX may use face recognition technology only for attendance verification where enabled by the institution. Facial data is used solely to verify the identity of registered students during attendance sessions. This data is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or misuse. Although we strive to keep your data secure, no online system can guarantee complete security.

5. Cookies

AttendX may use cookies and similar technologies to improve website performance, remember user preferences, maintain secure sessions, and enhance the overall user experience. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings if you prefer.

6. Third-Party Services

AttendX may use trusted third-party services such as fonts, icons, analytics, or hosting providers to improve functionality. These services may collect limited technical information according to their own privacy policies.

7. Children's Privacy

AttendX is designed for educational institutions. Student information is handled only for academic purposes under the guidance of the respective institution. We do not knowingly collect unnecessary personal information from minors.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in our services, legal requirements, or security practices. Any updates will be published on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions, suggestions, or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact the AttendX Team through the contact details available on our official website.
